Chapter 1, Spreadsheet Applications

Developing a spreadsheet application involves a structured process to ensure the final product is functional, user-friendly, and tailored to the intended audience.

  1. Define the purpose, objectives and scope of the problem.
  2. Develop user's requirements including common tasks and challenges by conducting user interviews.
  3. Develop a prototype to visualize layout, formulas and workflow.
  4. Develop and test the application, beginning with core functionality.
    Gather feedback.
  5. Document your efforts-decisions, formulas, etc. for maintenance and future reference.
    Create instructions and guides, but try to make the application self-explanatory.
